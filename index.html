Aaron Lou



I am an incoming second year computer science PhD student at Stanford University, where I am advised by Stefano Ermon. My research interests lie in the field of geometric deep learning, with a particular emphasis on developing generative models for non-Euclidean geometric data.

My research is supported by the NSF GRFP. Previously, I completed my bachelor's degree in mathematics and computer science at Cornell University, where I was advised by Chris De Sa.
